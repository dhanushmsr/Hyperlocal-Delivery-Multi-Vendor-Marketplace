Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

At FASTX (operated by OXRO Private Ltd), securing your personal data is our top priority. This policy outlines how we collect, store, and utilize data across our ecosystem.

1. Customer Privacy

  • Location Data: We require precise GPS coordinates (Latitude/Longitude) to accurately route your order to the nearest hub and provide accurate delivery tracking. This data is only actively polled while the app is in use.
  • Communication: Your phone number is masked where possible, but shared securely with Twilio for SMS/OTP verification, and temporarily with your assigned Rider to facilitate order drop-offs.
  • Financial Data: We do not store full credit card numbers. All FASTX Wallet transactions and online payments are encrypted and processed by PCI-compliant banking partners.

2. Seller (Vendor) Privacy

  • Business Data: We collect and store your Business Name, GST Number, FSSAI License, and Store Address to generate legally compliant tax invoices for your customers.
  • Financial Ledgers: Your sales data, platform fee deductions, and settlement history are logged securely on our internal servers to ensure accurate and transparent payouts.

3. Rider (Delivery Partner) Privacy

  • Live Tracking: To provide customers with live order tracking, we continuously collect and broadcast your GPS location only while you have an active delivery trip assigned to you.
  • Identity Verification: We securely store copies of your driver's license and vehicle registration solely for background verification and platform safety compliance.

4. Data Deletion & The "Right to be Forgotten"

FASTX empowers all users (Customers, Sellers, and Riders) with complete control over their digital footprint. By navigating to Account Settings > Danger Zone, you may initiate a permanent account deletion. Upon passing a secure SMS OTP verification, your profile, order history, and saved credentials will be permanently erased from our active databases.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is strictly shared with necessary functional partners, including OpenStreetMap (for reverse geocoding addresses) and Twilio (for SMS gateway delivery).
